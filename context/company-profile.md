# NexaFlow — Company Profile

## Overview
**NexaFlow** is a B2B SaaS workflow automation platform founded in 2021. It empowers teams to build, automate, and optimize business workflows without writing code.

- **Customers:** 3,000 active business accounts
- **Team Size:** 40 employees (fully remote-first)
- **Founded:** 2021
- **HQ:** Remote-first (no physical HQ); primary markets: United States, United Kingdom, Pakistan, India

## Pricing Plans

| Plan       | Price       | Users       | Support SLA                        |
|------------|-------------|-------------|------------------------------------|
| Starter    | Free        | Up to 5     | Best effort (community + AI)       |
| Growth     | $49/month   | Up to 25    | 24-hour response guarantee         |
| Enterprise | $199/month  | Unlimited   | 4-hour response + dedicated CSM    |

All plans include core workflow automation features. Enterprise adds API access, advanced analytics, SSO, and a dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM).

## Support Model
- **AI-first support:** 24/7 automated support via AI agent across all channels
- **Human escalation:** Available Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM PKT (Pakistan Standard Time)
- **Channels:** Gmail (email), WhatsApp (via Twilio), and Web Support Form (Next.js)

## Business Context
NexaFlow competes in the workflow automation and project management automation space. It targets operations teams, project managers, and engineering leads at SMBs and mid-market companies. Growth is primarily driven by product-led growth (PLG) with a self-serve motion, complemented by enterprise sales outreach.
